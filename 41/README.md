# Reference: Dancing Studios (studio)

Dance studios in the [Twin Cities](keg:pub/982) offer a vibrant community for
learning various dance styles. Whether you're interested in
[swing dancing](../33), [salsa](../26), [bachata](../27), or
[modern jive](../30), local studios provide instruction and social
opportunities for dancers of all skill levels. These spaces serve as gathering
points for the active dancing community, hosting regular classes, practice
sessions, and [social dance](../42) events that connect enthusiasts throughout
the [Minneapolis](keg:pub/932) and [St. Paul](keg:pub/981) area.
