# Cumbia (cumbia)

Cumbia is a Colombian partner dance and musical genre with Indigenous, African,
and Spanish roots. It has spread widely across Latin America, with regional
variations (Mexican, Colombian, Peruvian) that differ in footwork and style.

Should also be noted that this is Jose's favorite dance.

## Key characteristics

- Simple side-to-side or circular step pattern; accessible for beginners
- Distinctive shuffle or gliding footwork depending on regional style
- Partners typically dance in a slightly open hold
- Rhythm is 4/4 with an emphasis on beats 2 and 4
