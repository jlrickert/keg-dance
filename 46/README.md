# Cuban Salsa (cuban-salsa)

Cuban salsa, also called Casino, originated in Cuba and is characterized by circular movement patterns and Afro-Cuban
body movement rather than the linear slot used in LA or NY styles. It forms the foundation of [Rueda de Casino](../47).

## Key characteristics

- Circular, non-slot based movement; partners rotate around each other
- Strong Afro-Cuban influence: hip movement, shoulder shimmies, body isolations
- Hand tricks (manos) are a signature element
- Breaks on beat 1 like [on1](../43) but movement quality is quite different
