# Bachata (bachata)

Bachata is a Dominican partner dance danced to a 4-count rhythm with a hip accent (tap or pop) on beat 4. Movement is
typically side-to-side in open or closed hold. Modern styles (sensual bachata, urban bachata) add body waves and dips
borrowed from other dance forms.

## Key characteristics

- 4-count basic: side-together-side-tap, with hip movement on the tap
- Danced in open or closed hold; footwork stays relatively grounded
- Sensual/urban variants incorporate body isolations and dips
