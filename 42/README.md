# Social dancing (dance-social)

Social dancing is a vibrant community activity centered on partner-based dance
styles in social settings. The Twin Cities hosts numerous events and communities
dedicated to these dance forms, including regular meetups and themed nights at
local venues. Consider exploring the
[Twin Cities dancing event calendar](../35) for upcoming opportunities to
experience partner dancing in the region.

Social dancing encompasses partner-based dance styles performed in social
settings. This node serves as an index for various dance forms and communities
within the Twin Cities, including [swing](../33), [salsa](../26),
[bachata](../27), [jive](../28), and [modern jive](../30). It connects to
specific dance studios, events, and dancing communities that facilitate social
dancing experiences in the region.
