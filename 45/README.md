# On2 Salsa (on2-salsa)

On2 salsa, also known as New York style or mambo, breaks on beat 2 of the 8-count measure. This timing aligns with the
clave and the tumbao bass pattern in salsa music, which many experienced dancers prefer for its deeper musical
connection.

## Key characteristics

- Breaks on beat 2 (and 6); rhythm pattern: 2-3, 6-7 with pauses on 1 and 5
- Danced in a linear slot, same as [on1](../43)
- Considered more musically nuanced than on1; favored in New York social dance scenes
- Most on2 dancers also know on1, so switching between the two is common
