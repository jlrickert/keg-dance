# Merengue (merengue)

Merengue is the national dance and music of the Dominican Republic. It is one of the most accessible Latin partner
dances due to its simple marching-style footwork on a steady 2-count beat.

## Key characteristics

- 2-count march step: step-together or step-drag on each beat
- Hips move naturally as a result of the footwork, not forced
- Danced in closed or open hold; easy to lead turns and combinations
- Often used as an introductory dance because the basic step is intuitive
