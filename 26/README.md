# Salsa (salsa)

Salsa is a Latin partner dance originating from Cuban and Caribbean music traditions, danced to an 8-count rhythm with
breaks on specific beats depending on the style. It encompasses several distinct styles that differ in timing, footwork
pattern, and movement quality.

## Styles

- [On1 Salsa (LA style)](../43) — breaks on beat 1; linear slot; most common worldwide
- [On2 Salsa (NY style)](../45) — breaks on beat 2; linear slot; aligns more closely with clave
- [Cuban Salsa](../46) — circular movement; incorporates Afro-Cuban elements and hand tricks
- [Rueda de Casino](../47) — group circle format where a caller signals synchronized partner exchanges
